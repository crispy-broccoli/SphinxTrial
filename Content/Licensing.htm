Licensing

This topic will walk you through ROCK IMAGER FT license activations. If you have activated your license, you can always find your licensing information on the Licensing tab of the Report and Support menu.

Jump to:

  • Requesting a license key
  • Online activation
  • Manual activation

Note: You can continue enjoying ROCK IMAGER FT software even after your maintenance coverage expires, but you won’t have access to future software updates. For more information, please contact support@formulatrix.com.

Licensing Window

From the License Status area, you can see whether or not your software license has been activated.

  • License To: Identifies the account and user that this license is awarded to, along with the client ID.

  • License Product: Displays the name of the product being used.

  • License type(s): Every ROCK IMAGER FT purchase comes with an instrument license that encompasses all features . The instrument license is permanent, which means the software is yours for life.

  • License Feature(s): Displays all the features you have access to and what FORMULATRIX integrations are enabled.

  • Maintenance Expiration Date: Informs you the expiration date of your maintenance coverage for ROCK IMAGER FT.

License Activation

Follow the steps below to activate your license either online or manually.

Prerequisites:

  • You have your Client ID code.

  • You have your license key from FORMULATRIX support. If you do not have the license key, please read below on how to obtain it.

To request a license key:

  1. In the Report and Support menu, go to the Licensing tab.

  2. Select REQUEST LICENSE.

    Selecting Request License

  3. Choose one of the following options:

    • Online Activation: You must have an internet connection. ROCK IMAGER FT will attempt to communicate with the FORMULATRIX servers via the Internet.

    • Manual Activation: Alternatively, you can select this method to download a license request file. Send this file to support@formulatrix.com in order to receive the license key and complete the activation process.

      Note: In case you don’t have an active internet computer, you can copy the license request file to a different computer with internet access and then email support@formulatrix.com.

    Requesting a License

To activate your license via Online Activation:

  1. At this point, we assume you have acquired the required license key. In the Report and Support menu, go to the Licensing tab and select ACTIVATE.

    Selecting Activate

  2. In the License Activation dialog box, select Online Activation.

    Online License Activation

  3. Input the license key that you have obtained from FORMULATRIX support.

  4. Wait for a few moments while ROCK IMAGER FT processes your request. A notification will appear once your license has been successfully activated.

    License Successfully Activated

To activate your license by Manual Activation:

  1. Store the license key you received from FORMULATRIX on the ROCK IMAGER FT computer (e.g. the Desktop).

  2. In the License Activation dialog box, select Manual Activation.

    Manual Activation

  3. Click the Activate button and locate the license key file that you stored in your computer earlier. Then click Open, ROCK IMAGER FT will try to activate the license. A notification will appear once your license has been successfully activated.

    License Successfully Activated

Frequently Asked Questions:

► Will my ROCK IMAGER FT software stop working if the license expires?

Answer: FORMULATRIX software comes with a perpetual license that you own for life. Maintenance is not required to continue its use. However, you will receive an email notification 14 days before your ROCK IMAGER FT maintenance agreement is set to expire. This will give you time to extend your maintenance contract.

► What happens if my maintenance coverage expires?

Answer: You can continue enjoying ROCK IMAGER FT software even after your maintenance coverage expires, but you won’t have access to new features and bug fixes. For more information, please contact support@formulatrix.com.
