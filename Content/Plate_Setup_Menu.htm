Plate Setup Menu

The Plate Setup menu contains settings for all of your plate types. Upon installation, the FORMULATRIX support team will configure the properties for optimal image quality and performance. If you need a new plate type added to your imager, then please contact support@formulatrix.com for help. We only recommend advanced users modify these settings. The information in this menu is for lab managers so that they may adjust settings as they see fit.

Plate Setup Menu

The Plate Setup menu comprises three sub-menus:

  • Plate Type Calibration
  • Default Imaging Settings
  • Image Mask

In all of these sub-menus, the manual imaging controls are available in the Control Panel area to help you navigate between the wells and manipulate the optics and camera settings without having to go to the Imager menu.
