Welcome to ROCK IMAGER Flexible Temperature® Crystallization Imager Help

Welcome to the help file for the ROCK IMAGER FT (Flexible Temperature) 260, an automated system for imaging crystallization plates on a user-defined schedule. This help center contains detailed information on how to use ROCK IMAGER FT software and hardware, starting from loading and unloading plates to performing more advanced plate management tasks, including fine-tuning drop location on a per-plate basis and applying image masks.

If you can’t find what you are looking for, don’t hesitate to contact FORMULATRIX support at support@formulatrix.com.

Key Features

Highest reliability and plate density in the industry. The ergonomic design is built to accommodate up to 270 SBS plate types and more than 500 LCP plates.
Equipped with leading-edge temperature distribution and fluctuation control from 30° C down to 4° C, ensuring that your crystallization plates are always stored at the perfect temperature.
Offers multiple imaging modes to choose from: Visible light, UV (optional), Single Light Path (optional), compound zoom UV (optional).
Fully self-customized image scheduling.
Nightly diagnostic reports are sent to FORMULATRIX for continued quality control.
Linbro plate imaging compatibility with a dedicated slot in the Load Port.
